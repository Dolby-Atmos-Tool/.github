## 🧠 Overview

**Dolby Atmos** is an advanced spatial audio technology that delivers immersive, three-dimensional sound for entertainment and gaming.

It enhances audio positioning to create a more realistic listening experience through compatible headphones and speaker systems.

The technology improves clarity, depth, and directional accuracy across movies, games, music, and supported applications.

Perfect for gamers, movie enthusiasts, music lovers, and Windows users seeking premium audio quality.
